עליכם שלום!

Please note: سلام (salam) and שָׁלוֹם (shalom) in Semitic languages mean more than "peace". A better translation of the traditional greating and the response above:

- May there be a blessing of good order (and peace) upon you!
- And you all too be blessed with good order (and peace)!
